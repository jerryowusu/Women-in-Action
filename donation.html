How Your Donation Helps

Your generous contribution supports our programs that empower women through education, skills training, and mentorship. Here's how your donation can make a difference:

  • $50 provides learning materials for one student
  • $100 funds a week of coding classes for a participant
  • $500 sponsors a full month of training for a woman in our tech program
  • $1000 helps us expand our programs to reach more women in rural areas

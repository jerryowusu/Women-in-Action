Our History

Women in Action was founded in 2005 with a vision to empower women and girls across Ghana. Over the years, we have grown from a small community initiative into a nationwide movement, impacting thousands of lives through education, training, and advocacy. Our journey began with the belief that when women thrive, entire communities prosper.

Our Mission

To provide resources, guidance, and vocational training that empower women and girls to achieve their full potential and contribute to building stronger, more resilient communities.

Our Vision

A world where women and girls have equal opportunities to thrive, lead, and inspire others, creating a cycle of positive change for generations to come.

Empowering Women in Tech: Our Latest Initiative

Ama Mensah

Published on May 15, 2023

At Women in Action Rising, we're constantly looking for new ways to empower women and girls in Ghana. We're excited to announce our latest initiative: a comprehensive program aimed at increasing women's participation in the tech industry.

Why Tech?

The tech industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors globally, offering high-paying jobs and opportunities for innovation. However, women remain underrepresented in this field, especially in Ghana and across Africa.

Our new program seeks to address this gap by providing young women with the skills, mentorship, and opportunities they need to succeed in tech careers.

Program Details

  • 6-month intensive coding bootcamp
  • Mentorship from women in tech leadership positions
  • Internship placements with tech companies
  • Entrepreneurship training for tech startups

We're partnering with leading tech companies and educational institutions to ensure our program provides cutting-edge training and real-world opportunities.

Success Stories

Already, we're seeing incredible results from our pilot program. Akua, one of our first participants, shares her experience:

"Before joining this program, I never thought a career in tech was possible for me. Now, I'm working as a junior developer at a startup in Accra. This initiative has truly changed my life."

Get Involved

We're currently accepting applications for our next cohort. If you're a young woman interested in tech, or if you know someone who might be, we encourage you to apply or spread the word.

For companies and individuals interested in supporting this initiative, we offer various partnership and sponsorship opportunities. Your support can help us expand the program and reach more women across Ghana.

Together, we can create a more diverse and inclusive tech industry in Ghana and beyond. Join us in empowering the next generation of women in tech!
